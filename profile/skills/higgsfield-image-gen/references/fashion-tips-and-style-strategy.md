# Fashion Tips & Content Strategy Notes

## From YouTube Video: "How to Build Better Outfits" by Danie (shared by Fernando, May 27 2026)

### Key Takeaways for Lana's Content Strategy

**1. Get Inspiration & Study Patterns**
- Don't just scroll Pinterest — STUDY it. Notice patterns, silhouettes, colour schemes.
- Create mood boards of what you like. A 5-minute Pinterest scroll can inform future content.
- Follow fashion creators and incorporate their style elements.

**2. Capsule Wardrobe / Invest in Basics**
- Plain tank tops, good jeans, crop shirts, everyday pants, jackets, cardigans.
- Mix and match one pair of pants with different tops for completely different outfits.
- Content implication: fewer pieces = more variety when you know how to style them.

**3. Art of Layering**
- 2-3 layers is usually enough; too many looks messy.
- Layer jewelry (necklace stacking) for added detail.
- Thermal leggings under skirts for cold weather; loose breathable layers for hot weather.

**4. Know Your Body Proportions**
- Short torso = avoid high-waisted pants, avoid certain crop top lengths.
- Long torso = high-waisted works better.
- Content implication: know what cuts flatter the locked body proportions.

**5. Accessorizing = The Final Layer**
- Key charms on bags add personality/context.
- Hair accessories (berets, beanies, headbands) prevent "boring" hair-down looks.
- Signature jewelry becomes identity (e.g., a necklace worn with every outfit).

**6. Confidence is Key**
- Act confident even when not used to the outfit yet.
- People notice energy more than the outfit itself.
- Rewire perspective: they're admiring, not judging.

### Application to Lana's Content
- Build a capsule wardrobe of reference outfits that work for different shoots.
- Use layering to add variety without buying new pieces.
- Accessorize to make outfits feel more "intentional" for content.
- Match outfits to season/weather (no fuzzy fleece in summer).
- Create mood boards for signature looks.

## Seasonal Outfit Awareness (User Correction, May 27 2026)

Fernando pointed out that the cream/off-white fuzzy fleece set would be too hot for late May/June in Galway. 

**Rule:** Always consider the current season and weather when selecting outfits for content. Match the outfit to the time of year:
- Spring/Summer (Apr-Sep): Lightweight fabrics, camis, cropped tops, light joggers, breathable materials.
- Autumn/Winter (Oct-Mar): Velour sets, fuzzy fleece, layered looks, cozy pyjamas.

This applies to both prompt descriptions AND the product images selected as outfit references.
