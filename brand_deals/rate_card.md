# Lana's Rate Card

What Lana charges for brand collaborations. These are starting rates — adjust as her following and portfolio grow.

## Deal Types

### Gifted (Product Only)
Brand sends free product, Lana creates content.
- **Rate**: €0 cash, product value varies
- **When to accept**: Building portfolio, brand she genuinely loves, first collab with a small brand
- **When to decline**: Brand wants extensive deliverables for free product only
- **Deliverables**: 1-2 posts/stories, honest review

### Affiliate / Commission
Lana earns a percentage of sales through her unique link/code.
- **Rate**: 5-20% commission + sometimes a small base fee
- **When to accept**: Products she'd naturally recommend, brands with good conversion rates
- **Deliverables**: Posts with affiliate link, stories with swipe-up, ongoing promotion
- **Note**: Best for brands where her audience is likely to purchase

### Paid UGC (Usage Rights)
Brand pays for content they can use in their own ads/channels.
- **Rate**: €150-500 per asset set (3-5 photos OR 1-2 short videos)
- **+30% for whitelisting** (brand can run ads from her account)
- **+50% for exclusivity** (she won't work with competitors for X months)
- **+100% for full buyout** (brand owns content forever, can use anywhere)

### Paid Post (Sponsored Content)
Lana posts on her own channels, brand pays for the placement.
- **Instagram Feed Post**: €100-300
- **Instagram Story Set (3-5 stories)**: €50-150
- **Instagram Reel**: €150-400
- **TikTok**: €150-500
- **Bundle (1 feed + 3 stories + 1 reel)**: €250-600
- **Rates scale with follower count and engagement**

### Ambassador / Ongoing
Long-term partnership — monthly retainer for regular content.
- **Rate**: €300-1000/month for 2-4 posts + stories
- **3-month minimum**: €900-3000 total
- **6-month**: 10-15% discount on monthly rate
- **Includes**: Exclusivity in category, first look at new products, brand mention in bio

## Rate Factors

When negotiating, these factors push rates up or down:

### Push Rates UP ↑
- Brand wants exclusivity (no competitor collabs)
- Brand wants full content ownership / buyout
- Tight deadline (<7 days)
- Complex shoot requirements (specific location, props, multiple outfit changes)
- Brand is large/established with known creator budgets
- Content includes face/body prominently

### Push Rates DOWN ↓
- Lana genuinely loves the brand and wants the relationship
- Brand is small/independent — building portfolio value
- Simple deliverables, minimal requirements
- Long-term deal with guaranteed monthly income
- Product is high-value (compensates for lower cash)
- First collaboration — introductory rate

## Rate Tiers by Follower Count

| Followers | Tier | Feed Post | Reel | Bundle |
|-----------|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 1k-5k | Nano | €50-100 | €75-150 | €100-200 |
| 5k-20k | Micro | €100-250 | €150-350 | €250-500 |
| 20k-50k | Mid | €250-500 | €350-750 | €500-1200 |
| 50k-100k | Rising | €500-1500 | €750-2000 | €1200-3000 |

Lana starts at Nano/Micro tier and moves up.

## When Email Is Live

- Include rates in pitch only if the brand asks or if it's a paid UGC platform
- Otherwise: "Happy to share my rate card" — makes them ask, shows professionalism
- Never give a single number — give a range: "€150-300 depending on deliverables"
- Always specify what's included (usage rights, exclusivity, timeline)
