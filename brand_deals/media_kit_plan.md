## What She Needs Before Creating It

1. **Real content library** — enough photos/videos to show her range
2. **Follower count** — even small numbers are fine, just be honest
3. **Engagement data** — screenshots of post insights, story views
4. **At least 1-2 brand relationships** — even gifted collaborations count
5. **Professional photos** — at minimum 3-5 high-quality images of her

## Creation Options

When ready, media kit can be:
- **Canva template** — easy to edit, professional look
- **PDF** — standard format brands expect
- **Instagram Story highlight** — quick version for DM pitches
- **Link in bio page** — always accessible

## Pitch Deck vs Media Kit

- **Media kit**: Static, about Lana generally. Send when brands ask "tell me about yourself."
- **Pitch deck**: Custom per brand. Specific ideas for THIS brand, why THIS collaboration. Send when proactively reaching out.

She'll need both eventualmente. Media kit first, pitch deck templates second.
